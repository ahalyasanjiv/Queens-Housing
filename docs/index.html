Background

Willets Point is an industrial neighborhood near Citi Field that is currently going under redevelopment. The neighborhood previously was home to hundreds of auto shops, many of which have recently relocated from the neighborhood after being bought out to make room for the redevelopment project. The problem with the current plan for redevelopment is that many of housing units that are planned to be built will not be affordable for lower income residents. Willets Point sits within Corona, which has a median income of $46,493. If the redevelopment plan does not consider this, it may push current residents out of their neighborhoos. And so, our team aims to bring affordable housing to this area to create a better neighborhood for all.

Our Plan

[*Temporary*] The area is currently a C4-4 commercial zoning district, and we would want to change this to an R5 district to allow for more residential density. Willets Point is a triangle, and we would like to add housing to the inner triangle, slightly away from the 7 train, but close enough to have good access to transit. On the outer triangle, we would add commercial area, perhaps maintain some of the stores that exist. Ideally, we are looking the Flushing and Corona area to imitate their layout, since the demographics are similar.
